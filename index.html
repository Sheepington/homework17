Hi there! My name is Anna Andersen.

I’m a writer, editor, translator, and ux designer based in Austin, Texas.

I grew up in San Diego, California. Shortly after graduating from UCLA with a bachelor’s in communication studies, I moved to Reykjavik, Iceland, where I completed a master’s in journalism and worked as a journalist and culture magazine editor for many years.

Upon moving back to the States, I grew increasingly interested in ux design, and I realized that my strong research, interview, and storytelling skills were especially well suited to the field. I took an intensive, six-month ux design course through The University of Texas McCombs School of Business to learn the technical skills and began to pursue this new passion.
